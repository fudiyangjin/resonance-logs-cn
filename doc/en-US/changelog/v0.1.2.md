### Live Monitor

- Shield buff detail panel (Live Monitor → Enable Overlay → HP/Shield area) @18870
- Added Crimson CD
- Fixed Rock Heart talent counter reset from Rock Bullet Fury / free Giant Rock Wrath talents
- One-click potion listen adds resistance and enhancement potions
- New true factors

- Clear stored attributes on zone change—avoid stale values when full sync misses attributes

## Notes

### Compatibility

- If you used 0.0.2 or 0.0.3, first launch on 0.0.4–0.1.2 requires deleting `resonance-logs-cn.db` under `%LOCALAPPDATA%\resonance-logs-cn` and restarting
- Prefer Npcap; WinDivert signature expiry may cause AV false positives
