# History

Corresponds to **DPS Meter → History**.

History shows richer, more detailed review data than live stats, for example:

- **Damage breakdown per target**: Skill damage split by target (boss, trash, etc.)
- **Skill distribution details**: Expand a skill to see multiple damage sources beneath it
- **Full encounter data**: Summary stats for the entire fight

![History example](../../../shared/img/dps/dps_3.png)

- When history exceeds 200 entries, the next launch auto-removes older records by time and resets the sequence—no manual maintenance needed.

Training dummy mode also writes the current round to history on **end**; for daily review you can use the frozen Live view, and open history when comparing multiple runs. See [DPS Overview · Training Dummy Mode](./README.md#training-dummy-mode).
